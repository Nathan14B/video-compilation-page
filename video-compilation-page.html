Classical Music

The classical music is an important style of music in Western history. It is characterized by instrumental songs, usually played by an orchestra and, sometimes, vocal songs chanted by lyrical singers.

Johann Sebastian Bach

A german compositor of the Baroque period, well known by his harmonic arrangements and considered the patron of classical music.

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

One of the most famous classical compositors, is known to have been a prodigy since childhood.

Ludwig Van Beethoven

Maybe the greatest compositor in history, Beethoven is known for works such as Fur Elise, Moonlight Sonata and the Ode an die Freude of 9° symphony.
